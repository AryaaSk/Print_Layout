An app designed to make it easy to layout and print images of different sizes

When you open the app, you will have a blank canvas.

You can import an image by clicking the + icon, then importing using your selected method (paste, camera, gallery). Then you should be able to resize and move the image by dragging on it.

Finally when you are finished you can click the print button in the top-right, and it will open a print menu, you should ideally choose A4 size since that is what the app was designed for.

This app requires an internet connection.
